RoverCore is designed for beginner to intermediate ASP.NET core developers to learn how to develop ASP.NET SaaS applications. The project is a .NET 6 monolithic architecture that incorporates Entity Framework. The layered approach is intended to be simple and bare-bones, allowing the developer to decide what levels of abstractions they are comfortable with implementing.
